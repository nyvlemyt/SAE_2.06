Exhibition 2024

The Art of Nature

The exhibition "The Art of Nature - Creation and Sustainability" is an upcoming event at the Fondation Louis Vuitton in Paris that will run from June 1, 2024 to September 30, 2024. This exhibition is in line with current trends in contemporary art that celebrate the return to nature, and will focus on the relationship between contemporary art and nature, as well as environmental sustainability.


Exhibition program

The exhibition program includes an introduction to the relationship between contemporary art and nature, as well as a section exploring how nature serves as a source of inspiration for artists. There will also be a presentation of the different artistic techniques used to represent the environment, as well as exhibitions of sculptures and installations that embody the principle of sustainability. A specific part of the exhibition will be dedicated to artworks that place sustainability and the environment at the center of creation, thus highlighting the importance of nature conservation in contemporary art.

Schedule

The exhibition will be open from Tuesday to Sunday from 09:00 to 18:00, with no closing days during the exhibition period. Accompanied children under 12 years of age will be admitted free of charge, while adults will have to pay an admission fee of 15 euros.

Context

The reason for choosing such a subject for this exhibition is that it represents an artistic response to the current environmental crisis. By highlighting the interaction between art and nature, it aims to raise public awareness about the importance of environmental protection and demonstrate how art can contribute to this awareness.
