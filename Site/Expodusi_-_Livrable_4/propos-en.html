About

Who are we ?

We are an organization: EXPOSUDI. Since 2007, that is to say since the creation of the Louis-Vuitton company, we have been collaborating with this company. Especially our team, it started in 2014.


Why are the posters in English?

We make the posters in English, because there is a large part of our visitors who only speak English. From this, we reach a majority of people. In addition, the posters are explained in French.


The website

The EXPOSUDI site changes every year. The Event section represents last year's exhibitions. In the Team section, you can find the names of each person who worked for this project. If you want to change the language to English or French you have to go to the home page then you just have to click on the FR/EN button.
