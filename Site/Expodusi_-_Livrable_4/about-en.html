BASQUIAT x WARHOL, FOUD HANDED

Exhibition

The "Basquiat x Warhol" exhibition was held at the Louis Vuitton Foundation in Paris in 2023. This event celebrated the works of two icons of contemporary art, Jean-Michel Basquiat and Andy Warhol. Their influence on the art world was highlighted through this exhibition which showcased their significant contributions to a wide audience.

The exhibition featured a wide range of works by Basquiat and Warhol, including lesser-known pieces, giving visitors an in-depth understanding of their work. The works have been contextualized to emphasize their roots in African and Asian culture, in line with contemporary trends in art.

Program

The exhibition program included biographies and statements from the artists and curators, as well as information on events, workshops and lectures related to the exhibition. Visitors had the opportunity to better understand the artistic vision of Basquiat and Warhol, as well as their impact on contemporary art.

Schedule

The Louis Vuitton Foundation was open from Tuesday to Sunday, from 9:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m., with no closing days during the exhibition. Entrance was free for accompanied children under 12, and the entrance price for adults was 15 euros.

Author

Basquiat and Warhol were chosen for this exhibition because of their major role in contemporary art. These artists both left lasting legacies and were deeply influential.
